Fitness

When I was younger, I was not very active and very skinny, leaving me with self esteem issues growing up. When I reached high school, I started working out, where I started seeing myself progress more and more throughout my high school career. While I wasn't as strong as mypeers, I was happy to see improvement with myself, which is why I continued to workout, until COVID hit. One pandemic later, I lost my motivation to continue to go to the gym, and going into college, I'd occasionally go, but not at a consistent rate to see growth in myself. This year, currently my second year, I've gone every day, and while I'm not at the same spot as I was last year, I've loved seeing improvement in myself while I go to the gym everyday.
